1. Information Gathering

The information gathering stage is the crucial first step in the web development cycle, focused on understanding the project's core goals, target audience, scope, and client's vision before any design or coding begins, establishing the foundation for the entire project by defining why, who, and what the website needs to achieve to succeed. Clear documentation of these elements ensures the development team works with purpose and clarity. This phase prevents miscommunication and builds a strong foundation for every subsequent decision. Without it, teams risk developing irrelevant features or misaligned layouts. All stakeholders should sign off on the collected data before the next phase begins.
