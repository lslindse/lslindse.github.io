Stragety

The strategy stage is where the blueprint is made,ensuring everything that follows (design, development, launch, etc.) is built on a solid, goal oriented plan, preventing costly changes later on. The team uses the information they gathered and distinguishes what is important/needed based on what the client wants, they come up with how they are going to go about the website.
