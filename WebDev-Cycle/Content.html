3. Content Writing/ Assembly

This stage is crucial for creating and gathering all the text, images, and media that will be used on the website. The primary goal is to put in writing the core message you want to communicate to your target audience. This includes developing the site's voice, tone, and overall messaging strategy. Responsibilities include writing original content for pages, blog posts, and descriptions, editing and proofreading to ensure there are no mistakes, compiling and formatting existing content and media, and encouraging user interaction.
